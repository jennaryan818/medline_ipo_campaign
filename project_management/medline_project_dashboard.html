Risk Management & Mitigation Strategies

Critical Risk Assessment

Vendor Delivery Delays HIGH
Risk: Technical development or video production delays
Impact: Missed launch deadline, investor confidence loss
Mitigation Strategy:
  • • Contract backup vendors for each service
  • • Daily progress check-ins with all vendors
  • • Penalty clauses for delays in contracts
  • • 20% buffer time built into timeline
Compliance Violations HIGH
Risk: SEC or regulatory compliance issues
Impact: Legal liability, project shutdown
Mitigation Strategy:
  • • Pre-approved legal review of all content
  • • Securities lawyer on retainer
  • • Compliance checklist for all materials
  • • Regular legal team consultations
Budget Overruns MEDIUM
Risk: Costs exceeding allocated budget
Impact: Resource constraints, quality compromises
Mitigation Strategy:
  • • Fixed-price contracts with all vendors
  • • 15% contingency budget allocation
  • • Weekly budget tracking and reporting
  • • Pre-approved change order process

Risk Monitoring & Response Plan

Quality Control Issues MEDIUM
Risk: Deliverables not meeting quality standards
Mitigation Strategy:
  • • Multi-stage review process
  • • Quality checkpoints at 25%, 50%, 75%
  • • Independent quality assurance team
  • • Client approval required for each stage
Technology Failures LOW
Risk: Server outages, hosting issues
Mitigation Strategy:
  • • Redundant hosting infrastructure
  • • Automated backup systems
  • • 24/7 monitoring and alerts
  • • Disaster recovery procedures
